Why Presto?

Create a memorable experience and engage with guests.

Allowing guests to text reception makes interactions with the hotel more familiar and natural. Our goal isn't to cut the human touch out of the equation, but provide the best to interact with the hotel while guests aren't standing in the lobby.

Presto is efficent for your hotel since you don't have to respond to each message and get a greater level of control. We let you edit the wording of the responses to the most common requests and questions.

Any requests requiring hotel action, like a request for more towels, is sent to a simple dashboard accessible through provided tablets or any modern web browser.

How it Works

When a guest texts your hotel Presto uses computational linguistics to understand the intent behind message. This intent method is used because there are hundreds of ways to ask the same thing. We've mapped out the most common intents and have responses that are sent to the user.

All you have to do is set up a Presto number, we handle the rest.

FAQ

How much does this cost?
We charge a flat rate each month based off the number of rooms in your hotel. This includeds unlimited messages, so you can encurace your guests to use the service as much as they like.

What if Presto doesn't understand a guest's request?
Presto uses computational lingustics to understand the intent behind a user's request/question. We support hundreds of intents; however, if a guest asks something Presto doesn't understand they will recieve a polite message asking them to reach out to the front desk. The best part is Presto is constantly learning. Any question it's asked and doesn't understand will be quickly added in an supported.

How reliable is the service?
Presto is run entirely on Amazon Web Services servers. Those are the same servers that power Netflix, Amazon, and other major sites. We're super reliable.

Is Presto Multi Lingual?
Right now we only support English. However, support for other languages is coming soon. We have no technical limitation on the number of languages we can support.
